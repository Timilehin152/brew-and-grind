Our Story

About Brew & Grind

Born in Jamaica. Built on great coffee.

Who We Are

Brew & Grind was founded in 2019 by coffee enthusiast Marcus Reid in the heart of New Kingston. What started as a small espresso bar has grown into one of Jamaica's most loved specialty coffee chains — with three locations across the island.

We believe great coffee starts with great relationships — with the farmers who grow our beans, the baristas who craft each cup, and the customers who make it all worthwhile.

Every bean we use is responsibly sourced from Jamaican and Caribbean farms, roasted in small batches to preserve the natural flavour profile of each origin.
