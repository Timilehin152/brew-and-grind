1. What are your opening hours?

Our hours vary by branch. Kingston is open Mon–Sat 7am–9pm and Sun 8am–6pm. Portmore is open Mon–Sat 8am–8pm and Sun 9am–5pm. Spanish Town is open Mon–Fri 7:30am–7pm and Sat–Sun 8am–5pm. ↑ Back to top

2. Where are your branches located?

We have three branches: Kingston at 15 Knutsford Blvd, New Kingston; Portmore at the Portmore Mall, St. Catherine; and Spanish Town at White Marl Shopping Centre, St. Catherine. ↑ Back to top

3. How does the loyalty rewards program work?

Register for a free customer account and earn 1 point for every J$100 spent. Redeem 100 points for a free drink of your choice. Points are tracked through your Customer ID issued at registration. ↑ Back to top

4. Do you offer dairy-free or vegan options?

Yes! We offer oat milk, almond milk, and coconut milk as substitutes in all our drinks. Our Cold Brew Reserve and several pastries are also fully vegan. Ask your barista for details. ↑ Back to top

5. Can I place a catering order?

Yes, we do catering for corporate events, weddings, and private functions. Please email us at info@brewandgrind.com at least 5 business days in advance to discuss your requirements. ↑ Back to top

6. How do I register for a customer account?

Visit our Registration page, fill in your details, and submit the form. You will instantly receive a unique Customer ID which gives you access to the members menu and starts tracking your loyalty points. ↑ Back to top

7. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash (JMD), major debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), and mobile payments via Lynk. We do not currently accept USD at all locations. ↑ Back to top

8. Do you sell whole bean or ground coffee?

Yes! Our retail coffee bags are available in-store in both whole bean and pre-ground options. We currently offer our Kingston Blend and Blue Mountain Signature in 227g and 454g bags. ↑ Back to top

9. How can I give feedback or make a complaint?

We welcome all feedback! Use our Suggestions page to share ideas or concerns. For urgent complaints, please email info@brewandgrind.com directly and we will respond within 24 hours. ↑ Back to top

10. Do you offer Wi-Fi at your locations?

Yes, free Wi-Fi is available at all three Brew & Grind locations. Ask your barista for the current network name and password when you visit. ↑ Back to top
